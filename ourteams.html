Development Team

Having a young and dedicated team committed

A set of 3-9 people who synergically develop a product is said to
be development team. The Development Team in Scrum,
consists of two core characteristics. They are cross-functional
and self-organized. Cross-functional is nothing but the team has
all the skills required for a product development.

Cross-functional team of people are together responsible for all
of the work needed to produce working and validated assets.

This development team, work together towards a common goal.
Each member of this team is valuable to the common goal in a
different particular way, using a special set of skills to fulfil a
team role. Still, everyone on the team work with the same
orientation and attitude.

as an input value to verify its functionality. Then, the testers test
whether the given function is the suitable input in producing
the expected output.
